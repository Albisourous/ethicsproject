Mission Statement:


Companies have long collected and used private data on their customers hoping that they would be none the wiser. People all over the world are vulnerable to these practices simply because of a lack of information. Our goal is to educate internet users so that they have a fighting chance against these malicious tactics.


Who are we:


We are UT students on a mission to protect your privacy.
